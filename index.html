WHY ICSE?

ICSE Curriculum aims to ensure that learning is a joyful experience for all children and those children are able to understand not only what they learn, but also how this learning is relevant in their lives. Both present and future.

CO-CURRICULAR ACTIVITIES

Every activity in the school life plays a significant role in the development of the students. It is essential part of the school life and helps in enhancing learning process of the students at school.

RELATIONSHIP WITH GOD

Students' personal relationship with God and their personal responsibilities to friends, family, church and community will be of primary concern.

ABOUT THE SCHOOL

Siamliana Memorial School was established in 2020 with an aim to make the Best but affordable education with Biblical foundation available in the City. The School is located in Zomi Villa (Former Govt. Aizawl Law College), Tuikual North, Aizawl - 796001, Mizoram and is following an ICSE curricullum. There is an Elementary School level education at present and hoping to be upgraded in the future for High School and Higher Secondary school level of education when the institution has an affiliation to the Board.

MOTTO: With God all things are possible

WHAT MAKES SIAMLIANA MEMORIAL SCHOOL DIFFERENT?

* A STRONG FOUNDATION : It is well-known fact that a strong foundation in the lower classes plays a vital role in forging life-long learning competencies. Therefore, the school is doing its best to enable children to be well-prepared and future ready and leading them in a progressive and phased manner to derive full advantage of the ICSE.

* ACADEMIC AND CHARACTER BUILDING : We desire to build in the pupils a life-long burning passion to learn and grow spiritually. It is one thing to want children to be successful and quite another to provide the academic and character training to ensure this accomplishment.

* PROFICIENCY IN ENGLISH AND HINDI: English language occupies a central place in the school curriculum as it is the medium of learning. Proficiency in the language is a pre-requisite for effective communication and knowledge acquisition. And as we live in India, Hindi - the official language of the country is equally important as English and therefore cannot be neglected either. So, the pupils will be train to have proficiency in English and Hindi.

* MASTERY IN MATHEMATICS : Pupils will have mastery in Mathematics by developing the Mathematical thinking and problem solving skills and apply these skills to formulate and solve problems and will also acquire the necessary mathematical concepts and skills for everyday life and for continuous learning in mathematics and related discipline.

* TRAINING OF THE FUTURE GENERATION : The school would prepare the future generation of learners to meet the challenges of an ever advancing knowledge-based society and a dynamically changing environment, it is imperative that children are equipped with a repertoire of skills and a positive attitude with a sensitized perspective to become successful citizens in a globally competitive society. etc.
